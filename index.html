Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 9, 2026

Tsunagi ("the App") is an image combining tool. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data the App collects and how it is used.

Data Collection

The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All image processing is performed entirely on your device. Your images are never sent to any server.

Advertising

The App displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect certain data such as device identifiers, IP address, and usage data to serve relevant ads. For more information, please refer to Google's Privacy Policy.

In-App Purchases

The App offers an in-app purchase to remove advertisements. Purchase transactions are handled entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not collect or have access to your payment information.

Third-Party Services

  • Google AdMob - for displaying advertisements
  • Apple App Store - for processing in-app purchases

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
